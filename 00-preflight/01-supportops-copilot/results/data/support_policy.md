# Support Policy

## Returns

- Products can be returned within 30 days of delivery.
- The customer must provide an order ID.
- Damaged products are eligible for replacement or refund.

## Refunds

- Refunds are processed within 5 business days after approval.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

## Shipping

- Shipping delays may occur during holidays or bad weather.
- Customers should wait at least 10 days before reporting a missing package.

## Account Issues

- Customers must verify their identity before account changes.
- Password reset links are sent to the registered email.

## Billing

- Duplicate charges should be investigated before issuing refunds.
- Customers may be asked for transaction details.

## Escalation

Escalate tickets when:

- The customer is abusive.
- Fraud is suspected.
- Payment disputes cannot be verified.
- The issue remains unresolved after two interactions.

## Never Promise

Support agents must never:

- Promise refunds before verification.
- Guarantee delivery dates.
- Share customer information.
- Ignore company policy.
